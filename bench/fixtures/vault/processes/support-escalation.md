# Entry Criteria

Escalate when a customer is blocked, the workaround is unavailable, or the SLA
is at risk.

# Steps

1. Capture customer impact and reproduction steps.
2. Attach logs or screenshots that do not contain secrets.
3. Assign the owning team and incident severity.
4. Send the customer-facing update from the support owner.
